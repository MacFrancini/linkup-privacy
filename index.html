Privacy Policy for LinkUp

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

This app, LinkUp, does not collect or store any personal information. Your data is used only to support app functionality and is never shared with third parties.

What We Use

  • Location (once, with permission) — used only to suggest meet-up spots between you and your friend.
  • iMessage Integration — preferences and results are shared only with the person you're messaging.
  • Google Places API — retrieves results based on your shared preferences and location.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not store or save any data.
  • We do not track you.
  • We do not sell or share your information.
